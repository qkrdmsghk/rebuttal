Perturbation ranking accuracy by pathway effect. For each of 44 Hallmark pathways (≥20 perturbations with valid GSEA), all 272 test perturbations are ranked by predicted NES and compared with ground-truth rankings via Spearman ρ. Left: overall distribution across pathways. Right: per-pathway comparison for selected pathways sorted by AdaPert advantage. AdaPert (mean ρ = 0.207) and TxPert (0.205) substantially outperform Linear (0.082). AdaPert shows notable advantages on Glycolysis, Bile Acid Metabolism, and mTORC1 Signaling pathways.

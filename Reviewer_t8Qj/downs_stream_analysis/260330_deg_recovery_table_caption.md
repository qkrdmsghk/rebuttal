DEG identification accuracy across methods on K562 test set (n=272 perturbations). Overlap@K: fraction of ground-truth DEGs (FDR < 0.05) recovered in top-K predicted DE genes. Direction Accuracy: fraction of DEGs with correctly predicted regulation direction. DEG Recall: fraction of true significant genes identified as significant by predicted profiles. P-values from two-sided Wilcoxon signed-rank tests, paired by perturbation (AdaPert vs. TxPert). Best values highlighted in red. AdaPert significantly outperforms TxPert on Overlap@100 (0.258 vs. 0.235, p = 3.6×10⁻⁸), Overlap@200 (0.250 vs. 0.225, p = 1.9×10⁻¹⁴), and Direction Accuracy (0.866 vs. 0.850, p = 7.3×10⁻³).

Systema evaluation on RPE1 unseen perturbation setting (n=461 perturbations). AdaPert achieves the best PΔ_pert (0.379) and PΔ20_ctrl (highest among perturbation-aware methods), with centroid accuracy of 0.691. TxPert follows at 0.357. MORPH achieves competitive PΔ20_pert (0.365) but lower perturbation-reference metrics. Linear baselines and foundation models (scGPT, scVI) lag substantially behind.

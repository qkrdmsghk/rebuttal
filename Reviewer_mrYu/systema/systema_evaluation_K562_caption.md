Systema evaluation on K562 unseen perturbation setting (n=272 perturbations, 12 methods). Five metrics shown: PearsonΔ with perturbation reference (all genes and top-20 DE genes), PearsonΔ with control reference (all genes and top-20), and Centroid Accuracy. AdaPert achieves the best performance across all five metrics (PΔ_pert=0.534, PΔ20_pert=0.599, centroid=0.810), substantially outperforming TxPert (0.483, 0.550, 0.779) and all other baselines. Notably, STATE (−0.054) and matching-mean (−0.119) produce negative PΔ_pert, indicating predictions worse than random on this dataset.

Systema evaluation on JURKAT unseen perturbation setting (n=446 perturbations). AdaPert achieves the best PΔ_pert (0.345), PΔ20_pert (0.380), and centroid accuracy (0.687). TxPert follows closely (0.313, 0.329, 0.675). MORPH is competitive on centroid accuracy (0.676) but lower on perturbation-reference metrics. Foundation models (scGPT, scVI) and STATE show limited effectiveness.

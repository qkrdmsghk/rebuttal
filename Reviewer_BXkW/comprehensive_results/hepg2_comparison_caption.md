Method comparison on HEPG2 unseen perturbation setting (n=496 test perturbations). AdaPert achieves the highest Pearson Delta (+5.5% over TxPert) and competitive performance across all metrics. HepG2 presents a challenging setting with sparse DEG distributions (median 32 DEGs per perturbation), yet AdaPert consistently outperforms baselines on both global and DEG-level metrics including DE Direction Match (+3.5%) and DE Spearman LFC (+9.8%).

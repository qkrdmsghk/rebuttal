Pathway recovery from predicted expression profiles via GSEA (MSigDB Hallmark 2020, 300 permutations) across 272 test perturbations. Left: Pathway Recovery Rate — fraction of ground-truth significant pathways (FDR < 0.25) also identified as significant in predictions. Center: NES Correlation — Pearson r between predicted and ground-truth Normalized Enrichment Scores across matched pathways. Right: Direction Accuracy — fraction of pathways with correctly predicted enrichment direction (up/down). Diamonds denote means; boxes span IQR.

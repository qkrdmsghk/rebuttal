Method comparison on K562 unseen cell line setting (n=1,087 test perturbations). Models are trained on RPE1, HEPG2, and JURKAT cell lines, then evaluated on the held-out K562 line. Fewer methods are available in this setting due to training requirements. AdaPert achieves the highest Pearson Delta (+8.2% over Perturb Mean) and best Discrimination L1, Overlap@50 (+5.5%), DE Direction Match (+8.9%), and DE Spearman LFC (+19.8%). This cross-cell-line setting demonstrates AdaPert's ability to generalize perturbation predictions to an entirely unseen cellular context.
